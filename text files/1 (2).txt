The graph shows the return on capital employed (ROCE) and return on net worth (RONW) for Galaxy Surfactants Limited started in 1986 over the past five years. 

Financial analysis:
Galaxy Surfactants Limited's ROCE has increased from 18.3% in FY 2019 to 25.5% in FY 2023. This indicates that the company is generating a higher return on the capital it employs. There could be a few reasons for this, such as:
• Increased profitability
• Improved efficiency
Galaxy Surfactants Limited's RONW has fluctuated over the past five years. It reached a high of 25.2% in FY 2020 and a low of 19.3% in FY 2022. This means that the company's return on the shareholders' equity has been volatile. 
There could be a few reasons for this, such as:
• Changes in profitability
• Changes in the company's capital structure
• Share buybacks
